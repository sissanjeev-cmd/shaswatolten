My Story

Curious engineer, lifelong builder.

As a kid, I loved taking apart gadgets and toys to understand how they worked. This curiosity led me to pursue a bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from Delhi Technological University (DTU). During my second year, I discovered my passion for robotics and AI, sparking a deep love for the field.

The idea of creating a robot with human-like movement and thinking abilities excited me, setting me on a journey I continue to this day. Through hands-on robotics projects, I honed skills in vision-based path planning, safe reinforcement learning, and mastered tools like ROS2, Python, C++, and MATLAB.

I served as a research assistant at institutions like IIT Delhi and Toronto Metropolitan University, contributing to AI-based control algorithms for Continuum robots.

After graduating, I joined Orangewood Labs where I built RoboGPT — an LLM-based tool that cut programming time by 50%. I then pursued a MASc at the University of Waterloo, with thesis focused on Vision-based Path Planning for Aerial Manipulators using Safe RL.

Now at ArenaX Labs as an ML Researcher and Engineer, I'm excited to keep pushing the boundaries of robotics and AI.
